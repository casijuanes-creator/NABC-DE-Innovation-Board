## What this is

This is not a framework to read, but more an environment to work in.

You load these files into a Claude Project and you get a functional Innovation Lab: a Virtual Board of Directors, two integrated strategic frameworks, and a structured way to develop and stress-test business ideas.

This is for those of you who have done the courses and know NABC, and practiced with Disciplined Entrepreneurship. This is where you use them — on your actual idea, with your actual constraints, at 11pm the night before a pitch if that's what it takes.
If you have not use them, use this frame as a way of learning through questions in your Claude Project. 

---

## Two frameworks, one environment

**NABC** (Curt Carlson, SRI International) is a diagnostic and communication tool. One question: is this value proposition compelling enough? Four components — Need, Approach, Benefits per Costs, Competition — applied with discipline and real numbers. SRI used this to turn 20 years of decline into breakthroughs including HDTV and Siri.

**Disciplined Entrepreneurship** (Bill Aulet, MIT) is an execution roadmap. 24 steps from idea to validated venture. Starts with the customer. Demands quantification at every step. Forces you to test the scariest assumptions before you commit serious resources.

Together: from "is this worth pursuing?" to "how do I actually build it?"

## A Virtual Board of Directors

Twelve personas modeled after iconic leaders — across strategy, operations, innovation, ethics, and execution. Each has a documented philosophy and a distinct voice. They challenge your assumptions, ask the questions you are avoiding, and give you perspectives you would not naturally generate alone.

The concept of an AI-powered virtual board was pioneered by Vipin Gupta (MIT Sloan Management Review, 2025). This implementation extends it specifically for entrepreneurial and strategic work, integrated with NABC and DE.

|---|---|
| **v_Steve** (Jobs) | Product vision, simplicity, user experience |
| **v_Satya** (Nadella) | Culture transformation, platform thinking, growth mindset |
| **v_Indra** (Nooyi) | Stakeholder balance, long-term transformation |
| **v_Meg** (Whitman) | Scaling operations, org structure, discipline |
| **v_Elon** (Musk) | First principles, 10x thinking, speed |

## What a real session looks like

**Explore a new idea from scratch**
> "I'm exploring a B2B SaaS model for mid-size professional services firms who struggle with client reporting. Help me think through this."

The board engages. Claude maps where you are in DE. Assumptions surface. You end with a clearer picture and a sharper next step.

**Stress-test a value proposition**
> "Challenge this ruthlessly: [your pitch]. What am I missing?"

v_Ben, v_Elon, v_Andy come in hard. You leave with the real weaknesses, not the polite version.

**Work through a DE step**
> "Let's build my beachhead selection. Three candidate segments."

Claude guides you through Step 2. Board members weigh in. You produce a defensible choice with documented reasoning.

**Generate a document**
> "Create an NABC for my idea targeting potential investors."

Five questions, one draft, two iterations. A document you can actually use.

**Run a diagnostic**
> "I've been building this for six months. Run a DE diagnostic and tell me what I have skipped or assumed."

Claude maps your current state against all 24 steps, ranks the gaps by risk, tells you what to do next.
